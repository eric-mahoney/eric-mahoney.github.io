JS30 - Day 1 - Drum Kit

Hi, welcome to day one of my JS30 projects. Today is a drum-kit made in JavaScript. You can either click on the buttons, or press the corresponding key to play the sound. You can also create your own beat at the bottom by listening to the drum pattern and then playing it back. Just click "start beat" and drum away, then play when you're ready!
